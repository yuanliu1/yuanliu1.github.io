Our paper on [Efficient Qubit Simulation of Hybrid Oscillator-Qubit Quantum Computation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09233) is now on arXiv!
In this work, we established a framework for simulating hybrid oscillator-qubit quantum processors on qubit-only systems through position encoding with only a mild polylogarithmic overhead in circuit volume -- an exponential improvement over prior Fock basis encoding approaches.
