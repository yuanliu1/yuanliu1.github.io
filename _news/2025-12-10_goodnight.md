Congratulations to Prof. Liu for receiving the [2025-26 Goodnight Early Career Innovator Award](https://news.ncsu.edu/2025/12/nc-state-announces-2025-26-goodnight-early-career-innovators/)! \\
The award was established in 2021 to support early-career faculty excellence and to recognize faculty whose scholarship clearly and substantively contributes to innovation and advancement in STEM or STEM education at NC State. Many thanks to the College of Engineering and the University for their generous support!
