#### Dr. Cheng Chu

Postdoctoral Scholar at NC State

Cheng received his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electrical Engineering from Hefei University of Technology in 2018 and 2021, respectively. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Indiana University Bloomington in 2025. His research primarily focuses on quantum computing, with particular emphasis on quantum computer architecture and variational quantum algorithms. [Email](mailto:cchu8@ncsu.edu)
