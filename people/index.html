Professor Yuan Liu

Assistant Professor, Electrical and Computer Engineering, Computer Science
(affiliated faculty with Physics)

Prof. Yuan Liu received his B.S. in Physics from Tsinghua University, a M.S. in Electrical Engineering and a Ph.D. in Chemical Physics (William R. Potter Prize, highest honor) from Brown University. He was a postdoctoral researcher in the Research Laboratory of Electronics and Department of Physics at MIT prior to joining the NC State faculty in 2024. He is the NSF QCIS Faculty Fellow, and holds a joint appointment across three departments at NC State. He is a Scialog Fellow and recipient of the ECE Rising Star Award. Email. CV.
